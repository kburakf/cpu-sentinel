# Privacy Policy

**CPU Sentinel** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All process monitoring is performed locally on your device. No data leaves your Mac. No analytics, no tracking, no network requests.

The app uses macOS system APIs (`sysctl`) solely to read process information for the purpose of detecting orphan processes. This data is never stored or shared.
